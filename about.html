Rangpur Haji Abul Hossain Institute of Technology

Introduction

Twenty first century has started its journey with a promise to reach the blessings of science and technology to every human being on the Earth. In such a perspective technical education has become most significant and new horizon of studies. To cope with the demand of the age, to achieve the millennium development goals (MDGs) the Government has given much emphasis on technical education. For this purpose Haji Abul Hossain Trust has been working uncompromisingly.

Location

The Institute is in its own campus at the heart of Tangail city located Masjid Road nearly Tangail central Mosque. It is a 9 storied building accommodating the class rooms, workshops & laboratories, Administrative offices, library, Labs, auditorium, Multipurpose common room & a Canteen . Located in Tangail, HABHIT is geared with the best infrastructure and facilities. Its Honors campus is situated at Hobibor Rahman Plaza, Old Bus stand Tangail.

Vision

HABHIT intends to expand technical education as the students can prepare them according to the country’s needs & demands and so that they can be technically educated leading towards progress.

Mission

The Mission of Haji Abul Hossain Institute of Technology is to provide quality service to the society in the field of dissemination of information technology gradually extending the network to the rural areas and is to produce highly skilled technical manpower. This mission is achieved by imparting high quality technical education and training so that individuals can achieve their intellectual, social and personal potentials.

Why Choose HABHIT?

On your journey to find the Institute that is right for you, we understand that you probably have many questions. At HABHIT, we want you to strive for a better education and a better future for yourself—and those you care about. You have a desire to make a difference, and so do we. We are not just focused on academics; Individual Attention, we make sure that you personally and professionally evolve. When you step out of HABHIT, we make sure you are ready to pursue your dreams by finding a unique career and experiencing a more fulfilling life.
